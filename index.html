Our Mission

Enactus UNBF is a student‑run social enterprise organisation based at the University of New Brunswick in Fredericton. We build entrepreneurial initiatives that create positive impact in our community and beyond.

Our History

Since our founding in 20XX, our team has launched multiple projects addressing education, sustainability and economic empowerment. We are part of the global Enactus network of students, academics and business leaders.

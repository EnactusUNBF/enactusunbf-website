Our Mission

Enactus UNBF is a student‑run social enterprise organisation based at the University of New Brunswick in Fredericton. We build entrepreneurial initiatives that create positive impact in our community and beyond.

Our History

Since our founding, our team has launched multiple projects addressing education, sustainability and economic empowerment. We are part of the global Enactus network of students, academics and business leaders.

About TreeFusion

TreeFusion is an open-source web app that simplifies plant growth data by linking reliable metrics with local climate insights, helping gardeners, farmers, and sustainability advocates adopt smarter, sustainable practices.

About Monevo

Monevo is a free, AI-powered financial literacy platform that helps university students manage debt, budgeting, and long-term planning through personalized guidance and gamified learning, empowering them to build financial confidence and stability.

About Carbon Black Technologies

Carbon Black Technologies uses advanced thermal plasma technology to convert plastic, oil, and biomass waste into high-value carbon materials and hydrogen-rich syngas, reducing emissions and waste while creating scalable clean energy and circular economy solutions.
